Research

ACM · NSysS 2025 · Sylhet, Bangladesh

Energy-Efficient Web Design: Measuring Impact of Front-end Optimizations

Jerin Anan Proma, F. T. Zannat, M. S. Aftab, T. A. Tripty, M. S. H. Khan, R. U. Islam, R. A. Tuhin

A controlled empirical study of how image compression, lazy loading, and code minification affect client-side energy use and CO₂ emissions — measured with a dual-instrument setup over 24 Selenium-automated browsing sessions. Combined techniques cut whole-device energy by up to 89.8%.
